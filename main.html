The Cloud...

...are a public access WiFi provider with thousands of hotspots and millions of registered users. The Cloud builds and operates WiFi networks across the UK and Europe to provide the mobile media experience that consumers want. The service delivers simple, fast, reliable and cost effective broadband to millions of mobile wi-fi enabled smartphones, laptops and entertainment devices.

Our hotspots are located throughout the UK in major cities and towns including London, Leeds, Manchester, Newcastle, Bristol, Cardiff, Glasgow and Edinburgh. You will find Cloud wifi hotspots located within well-known high street retailers, shopping malls, hotels, pubs, sports stadiums, leisure groups, train stations and entertainment venues.

Our partners that supply public wifi include – Emirates Stadium, Silverstone Race Track, the City of London, PizzaExpress, Caffe Nero, Eat, WH Smith, Greggs, Wetherspoons, Pret a Manger, Wagamama, Network Rail and First Great Western to name a few. Use our hotspot finder to find your nearest Cloud wifi hotspot.

The majority of our partners offer free wifi except stations and coaches which tend to charge after a limited time period.
