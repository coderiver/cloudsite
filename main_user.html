With the Cloud WiFi you can...

Get access to thousands of our public wifi hotspots throughout the UK plus we have hotspots in Germany, Denmark, Norway, Sweden and Finland. You will find them conveniently located within cafes, restaurants, pubs, shopping centres, high street retailers, hotels, entertainment venues, amusement parks and train stations.

Save money on your data allowance by using free wifi instead of 3G which eats into your mobile data allowance and get on with work, check out social networks whilst you’re out and about on your smartphone, laptop or tablet.

All you have to do to use our WiFi is register for a Cloud account via our free app, FastConnect, which makes finding your nearest hotspot easy, plus when you register via FastConnect your device will autoconnect to the Cloud WiFi so you don’t have to remember your username and password every time you want to connect to a Cloud hotspot.

If you’d rather you can also register when you are in a Cloud venue or register for a account today so that you’re ready to make the most of the WiFi when you go into a Cloud hotspot.

Get on the WiFi at high street retailers including WHSmiths, Ted Baker, M&S, BHS and cafes including Pret a Manger, Eat, Café Nerro, restaurants including PizzaExpress, Wagamamas, Loch Fyne and pubs including Wetherspoons, Lloyds and many more...
